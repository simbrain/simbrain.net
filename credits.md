## Tools & Libraries

This software uses various [Apache](http://www.apache.org/) tools, and the following libraries:

- [Beanshell](http://www.beanshell.org/)
- [Colt](http://acs.lbl.gov/%7Ehoschek/colt/)
- [GlazedLists](http://publicobject.com/glazedlists/)
- [JAMA](http://math.nist.gov/javanumerics/jama/)
- [JFreeChart](http://www.jfree.org/jfreechart/)
- [JGAP](http://jgap.sourceforge.net/)
- [Piccolo](http://www.cs.umd.edu/hcil/piccolo)
- [OjAlgo](http://ojalgo.org/)
- [RText and RSyntaxTextArea](http://bobbylight.github.io/RText/)
- [SSJ](http://mvnrepository.com/artifact/ca.umontreal.iro/ssj/2.5)
- [SwingLabs](https://java.net/projects/swinglabs)
- [XStream](http://x-stream.github.io/)

Some of the data (in particular the `.arff` files) were drawn from the [Weka](https://www.cs.waikato.ac.nz/ml/weka/) project, and from the website of [Gary Weiss](https://storm.cis.fordham.edu/~gweiss/data-mining/datasets.html). Some of the tools at [Ostermiller.org](http://ostermiller.org/) are also used. Bits of open source code found online or in books are noted in code documentation.

## Funding and Grant Support

The development of Simbrain has been supported by several grants, including an early grant (around 2005) from the Hewlett Foundation, and several UC Merced faculty research grants. Between 2016 and 2019 some work on Simbrain was supported by a grant from the National Science Foundation (An Analysis of the Consequences of Cortical Structure on Computation Award Number: 1513779).
